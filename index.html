About HUST

The Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST) is a prestigious technical and technological university based in Hanoi, Vietnam. HUST is regarded as one of the leading, largest engineering universities in Vietnam, under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Education and Training, and classified among the group of key national universities.

HUST EXAM

The HUST EXAM application aims to organize mid-term and final practice exams for SOICT students for subjects tested on computers. The tests are formatted similarly to the actual exams, enabling students to practice effectively. The question sets and questions are collected and verified from the professors and official materials.
